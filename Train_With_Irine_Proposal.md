**TRAIN WITH IRINE**

Landing Page Proposal

*Updated May 5, 2026*

**Project Overview**

Train With Irine is a Kerala-based fitness coaching brand for women who want serious, sustainable training with a real coach beside them. The landing page will act as the main digital touchpoint: it introduces Irine's authority, explains the coaching method, clarifies the three programme options, and uses a short quiz to help visitors choose the right level of support.

The tone is premium but warm: disciplined, personal, and grounded. The page should feel like private coaching, not a loud fitness funnel.

**Strategic Objectives**

- Build authority through Irine's coaching background, Miss Kerala 2024 experience, and powerlifting credibility.
- Reduce decision friction by explaining each programme clearly and matching visitors through a three-question quiz.
- Drive qualified Instagram DMs from visitors who understand the offer before they reach out.
- Preserve the Train With Irine brand system consistently across the website, proposal, and future assets.

**Page Structure & Key Features**

**1. Navigation**

- Train With Irine wordmark treatment with "get fit online" tagline.
- Links to About, Method, Programmes, and Quiz.
- Primary CTA to apply through Instagram.
- Mobile hamburger menu with large tap targets and clear close behavior.

**2. Hero**

- Editorial hero using the line "Personal coaching, reimagined."
- Supporting copy focused on one-to-one coaching, weekly calls, and adaptable training.
- Primary CTA: Find your programme.
- Secondary CTA: See all three programmes.
- Hero image is currently a placeholder until a real Irine hero photo arrives.

**4. Method**

- Five-part coaching promise: real coaching, accountability, flexible structure, sustained momentum, and confident movement.
- Visual tone should remain editorial and warm, using placeholder imagery only until real brand assets are supplied.

**5. About Irine**

- Irine Rose positioned as coach first, influencer second.

**6. Interactive Fit Quiz**

| Step | Question | Purpose |
|------|----------|---------|
| 1 | Primary fitness goal | Understand desired outcome |
| 2 | Current experience | Identify starting level |
| 3 | Desired support | Match the coaching intensity |

The quiz recommends one of the three programmes and then routes the visitor to Instagram DM. Validation should prevent users from advancing without choosing an option.

**Brand System**

The website must follow `Brand System.html` as the strict visual authority.

**Direction**

Editorial Warmth: refined, calm, personal, and feminine without becoming soft or generic. The brand should feel like a private coaching studio with discipline behind it.

**Typography**

- Display: Cormorant Garamond
- Body/UI: Manrope
- Labels/technical details: JetBrains Mono

**Palette**

| Role | Direction |
|------|-----------|
| Paper | Soft warm background |
| Ink | Primary text and dark sections |
| Clay | Warm highlight and soft accent |
| Terracotta | Conversion accent and selected states |
| Supporting muted tones | Used sparingly for section variety |

**Voice**

- Say: "A practice, not a punishment."
- Say: "Strong, on your terms."
- Say: "Coaching, not influencing."
- Avoid: crash-diet language, shame-based copy, fake urgency, exaggerated guarantees, or anything that implies results without work.

**Technical Specifications**

- Static HTML website.
- Tailwind CSS build through Tailwind CLI, compiled to a deployable CSS file.
- No React, Vite, Next.js, or runtime framework.
- Vanilla JavaScript for navigation and quiz behavior.
- Basic SEO, Open Graph, canonical, and JSON-LD placeholders included.
- Mobile-first responsive behavior with accessible tap targets.
- Playwright verification for desktop, mobile, navigation, anchors, and quiz flow.
